Kestrel Fold

Kestrel Fold builds inventory-forecasting software for mid-market grocery chains running 20-200 stores. Founded in 2016, we help operations teams cut overstock write-offs without hiring a data-science team.

What makes us different: our forecasts run on the point-of-sale data grocers already have, with no new hardware and no new POS integration.

Buying signal we look for: a chain that just missed a seasonal demand spike and is actively comparing forecasting vendors.
